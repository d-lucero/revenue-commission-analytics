# Dashboard Design Notes

## Dashboard Purpose

This dashboard is designed to support financial reporting, commission review, and month-end close analysis for a subscription-based services business.

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Financial Overview
- Total recognized revenue
- Total deferred revenue
- Monthly recurring revenue trend
- Revenue by customer segment

### 2. Commission Reporting
- Total commission expense
- Commission by sales representative
- Commission by region
- Commission-to-revenue ratio

### 3. Revenue Recognition
- Recognized vs deferred revenue
- Contract revenue schedule
- Revenue by contract term

### 4. Month-End Close Review
- Revenue variance flags
- Commission payout review
- Contracts missing revenue schedules
- High commission rate alerts

## Business Value

The dashboard helps Finance, Sales, and Operations teams improve visibility into revenue performance, commission accuracy, and recurring financial reporting cycles.
